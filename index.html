College Major Visualizations

CS416 Data Visualization Project

Overview of College Majors

Scene 1
In this scene, you can find the popularity of majors along with their unemployment rate and salaries.
Hovering over the circles shows more information about each major as well!

Insight into Job placement

Scene 2
Now you can see how well certain majors did in their job search after graduating. You can also see the amount of people in each major that
actually used their degree.
Hovering over the each bar will give you the percentage of graduates in that category.

Disparity in Males and Females in each Industry

❮
Scene 3
Now you can see how well certain majors did in their job search after graduating. Also how many jobs are on the market for these majors.
Hovering over the each bar will give you the the number of men and women. Click on the bars for a suprise!
